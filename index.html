🎓 Fun Shark Facts

🦴 Sharks have no bones!

Their skeletons are made of cartilage, like the tip of your nose!

👃 Sharks smell with their nose!

They can smell a drop of blood from miles away in the ocean!

📏 Sharks never stop growing!

Some sharks can live over 100 years old and keep getting bigger!

⚡ Sharks are faster than you!

They can swim up to 40 miles per hour, and some are even faster!
